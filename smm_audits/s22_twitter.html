2. Форматы контента

Single Tweets — 4 формата

A. Hot Take (наивысший engagement)
Unpopular opinion: Most "AI agents" are just if-else chains wrapped in an API call. Real agents need: persistent memory, tool selection, error recovery, autonomous decision-making. I run 6 of them in production. 90% of what I see on my timeline wouldn't survive a weekend.
B. Metric Drop (высокий bookmark rate)
My AI video pipeline costs: - $0.09/minute of video - 47 seconds generation time - 0 human editing The agency quoted me $500/minute. Here's the exact stack: [thread]
C. Screenshot/Demo — terminal output, dashboards, side-by-side. Tech Twitter loves real outputs.
D. Contrarian Question — "How many of your 'autonomous agents' actually run without intervention for 72h? I'll go first: 4 out of 6."

Threads — самый мощный формат

Генерируют наивысший total engagement. Optimum: 5-10 tweets. Hook tweet определяет 90% reach. Body tweets — independently valuable. Closer: CTA или engagement question.

Video — 3-5x algorithmic boost в 2025-2026

Twitter активно бустит видео (конкуренция с TikTok/Shorts). Screen recordings 30-90 sec, talking head + screen 2-5 min, demo reels 15-30 sec. Существующий AI video pipeline (shorts_v2.py, $0.09/мин) → near-zero cost репёрпозинг.

5. Tone of Voice — адаптация для Twitter

Twitter rewards opinion velocity

LinkedIn = polished thought leadership. Telegram = conversational warmth. Twitter = fast, sharp, opinionated takes backed by evidence. Контент должен быть dialogical — argue, debate, challenge, respond.

Builder's Log Voice — Tim's authentic Twitter voice

Day 47 of running AI bloggers in production. Today's surprise: the Threads API rate-limited us after 247 posts. Docs say 250/day. Reality: 247. Workaround: stagger posts across 2 tokens. Cost of the fix: 12 minutes of my time. These are the details nobody writes about.

Адаптации от существующего tone of voice

ПравилоTwitter адаптация
Не начинай с "я сделал"Twitter: "I" OK когда = proof-of-work ("I shipped", "I tested", "I benchmarked")
No messianic narrativesKEEP — cringe detection на Twitter ещё выше
Links в каждом постеMODIFY — links go in REPLIES, не main tweet (40-60% fewer impressions)
Дружелюбный тонSHARPEN — Twitter "friendly" более casual + допускает edge/sharpness

Что НИКОГДА не писать на Twitter

  • "I'm so excited to announce..." (cringe)
  • "Thread incoming!" без самого thread
  • "Please RT" / "share this" (begging = credibility kill)
  • LinkedIn-style длинные абзацы с эмодзи
  • "AI will take all jobs" doom (clash с SBORKA mission)

9. Cross-Platform Syndication

"One Idea, Five Formats" Framework

Одна идея ("AI agents cost $47/mo") → 1. Twitter: Hot take single tweet 2. LinkedIn: Professional breakdown (500 words) 3. Threads: Micro-take (500 chars) 4. Telegram: Разговорный пост (RU) 5. Dev.to: Full technical article (2000+ words)

Правила адаптации

DirectionПравило
Twitter → LinkedInExpand, add context, remove edge/sharpness, add "takeaway" framing
LinkedIn → TwitterExtract hottest take, strip corporate tone, add numbers
Twitter → ThreadsCompress to 500 chars, keep hook, add emoji sparingly
Twitter → TelegramTranslate to RU, add warmth, expand context
YouTube → Twitter30-sec clips from videos, screen recordings, key moments
What NOT to cross-post: Twitter hot takes → LinkedIn (too edgy). LinkedIn essays → Twitter verbatim (too polished). Platform-specific engagement bait (polls designed for one platform).
