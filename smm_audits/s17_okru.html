1. Algorithm & Feed Mechanics

OK.ru uses a hybrid chronological + recommendation feed that heavily weights social signals from the extended network (friends of friends, group members). The platform's unique viral mechanic is the "Class" (Klass) reaction cascade.

How "Class" Cascade Works

1 "Class" from User A → notification to ALL of A's friends → appears in THEIR feeds → more "Classes" → exponential reach
A post with 20 classes from 20 different people can organically reach 2,000-5,000 feeds. This makes OK.ru's viral mechanic fundamentally different from Instagram/VK likes, which only boost within the algorithm — on OK.ru, each class is a distribution event.

What Gets Boosted

  1. Content earning classes in first 30-60 min — the cascade window
  2. Native OK Video uploads — 3-5x more reach than YouTube links
  3. Group content — posts distributed to all group members' feeds
  4. Interactive formats — polls, quizzes, Moments (Stories analog)
  5. Daily active profiles — posting + commenting + classing = higher reach
External links in post body suppress reach by 30-50%. ALWAYS put links in comments. Also: duplicate cross-posted content, text-only posts, and low-friend-count profiles get suppressed.

3. Competitor Analysis — AI/Tech/Career on OK.ru

OK.ru has almost zero personal brands doing "AI for your career" in accessible language. The AI groups are either too technical (code tutorials) or too superficial (news reposts). Nobody bridges AI + career for 35+ professionals. This is the biggest blue ocean in Russian SMM.
"Programmirovanie i IT" (Group)
~180,000 members · Daily posting · Low quality
Largest Russian IT group on OK.ru. Quantity-focused memes and news. No thought leadership, no career focus. Lesson: Volume works on OK.ru, but quality niche is wide open.
"Iskusstvenniy Intellekt / AI" (Group)
~25,000 members · 3-5/week · Growing fast
No original content — just reposts from Habr/VC. Growing fast = demand exists, supply doesn't. Lesson: Original AI content in accessible language wins immediately.
HeadHunter (Official Group)
~500,000 members · Daily · Corporate tone
Closest career competitor. Job market news, resume tips, generic career advice. Corporate, broad, impersonal. Lesson: Personal voice + specific AI angle differentiates instantly.
"Rabota i Karera" (Group)
~90,000 members · Daily · High engagement on "life after 40"
Targets 30-50 age group perfectly. High engagement on age-relevant content. Lesson: "Career after 40" framing resonates deeply. Join this group first.
"Biznes-idei" (Group)
~300,000 members · Daily · Story-based content
Very active community. Content is simple, accessible, story-driven. Lesson: Storytelling >> technical depth on OK.ru. Share entrepreneurship angle.
SuperJob (Official Group)
~150,000 members · 3-4/week · Salary surveys
Professional but generic. Data-heavy (salary reports). Lesson: Data is valued. Incorporate job market analytics with AI spin.

DO on OK.ru

  1. Use "Vy" (formal "you") initially. Switch to "ty" after community trust is built
  2. Start with relatable problems, not tech solutions: "You've sent 50 resumes and got 2 responses. Familiar?"
  3. Use concrete numbers and outcomes. "Member found job in 3 weeks after 6 months of searching"
  4. Include personal warmth and backstory. "15 years in tech" credibility markers work well
  5. Explain EVERY tech term. "ChatGPT (this is an AI assistant, like a very smart colleague)" — not patronizing on OK.ru, it's expected
  6. Acknowledge the audience's life stage. "You've built a career. You have experience. But the market changed"

DO NOT on OK.ru

  1. No English terms without translation. "AI" is borderline OK. "Prompt engineering" = translate and explain
  2. No ironic/sarcastic tone. What works on Telegram may come across as disrespectful
  3. Never talk down. No "if you still don't know what ChatGPT is..." framing
  4. No abbreviations (IMHO, LOL, kek, etc.)
  5. Don't focus on "young/progressive" tools — focus on PRACTICAL and TIME-SAVING

Tone Adaptation Example

WRONG (Telegram style): "AI v nayme: kak ne prosrat' sleduyushchiy god. Rezyume na HH uzhe nikto ne chitayet. Neyronki delayut eto za rekruterov. Chto delat'? Prikhodite v SBORKU."
RIGHT (OK.ru adapted): "Znayete, chto izmenilos' v poiske raboty za posledniy god? Teper' vashe rezyume snachala chitayet iskusstvennyy intellekt, a uzhe potom chelovek. Eto ne fantastika. Tak uzhe rabotayet bol'shinstvo krupnykh kompaniy. Khoroshaya novost': k etomu mozhno podgotovit'sya. V nashem kar'yernom klube SBORKA my razbirayem, kak adaptirovat' rezyume pod AI-fil'try..."
