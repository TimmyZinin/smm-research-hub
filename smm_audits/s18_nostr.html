1. Nostr Protocol Mechanics

Nostr (Notes and Other Stuff Transmitted by Relays) is a decentralized protocol built on three primitives: Clients (apps), Relays (WebSocket servers), and Keypairs (secp256k1 cryptographic identity, same curve as Bitcoin). No blockchain, no token, no central authority.

Core Architecture

Private key (nsec) → signs events → Public key (npub) = your identity → NIP-05 = human-readable (tim@sborka.work)

Key Event Kinds

KindNameRelevance
0Profile MetadataHIGH — first impression, name, bio, NIP-05, Lightning address
1Short Text NoteHIGH — daily posts (like tweets, up to ~65KB)
6RepostHIGH — sharing others' notes (engagement)
7ReactionHIGH — likes, emoji reactions
9734/9735Zap Request/ReceiptHIGH — Lightning micropayments on notes
10002Relay List (NIP-65)HIGH — tells network where to find your content
30023Long-form ContentHIGH — full Markdown articles (blog posts)

Content Propagation Model

Unlike centralized platforms, there is NO single algorithm. Each client implements its own feed logic. Primal has trending feeds, Damus is more chronological. Your relay selection and network effects matter more than "gaming the algorithm." Getting reposted (kind 6) by someone with wider relay coverage is the primary viral mechanic.
Events are immutable once published (no edit). No guaranteed delivery if relays go down. No global search (relay-dependent). Images need external hosting (nostr.build, void.cat). These are protocol tradeoffs for censorship resistance.

5. Lightning Network & Zap Strategy

Zaps are instant Bitcoin micropayments attached to specific notes — the killer feature differentiating Nostr from every other social platform.

Lightning Address Setup

Phase 1: Start with Alby (getalby.com) — free hosted wallet, easy setup.
Phase 2: Migrate to LNbits on RUVDS — full sovereignty, custom Lightning address tim@sborka.work.
Set lud16 in Nostr profile metadata to enable receiving zaps.

Strategic Value of Zaps

  1. Social proof: Zap counts visible on posts signal quality (like likes but with real money)
  2. Community building: Zapping others builds relationships and visibility
  3. Demand validation: If AI content earns meaningful zaps, it confirms market
  4. Gateway to services: "If you like what my agents do, imagine them working for YOUR business"
CRITICAL cultural rules: Say "sats" not "dollars." Say "Bitcoin" not "crypto." NEVER mention altcoins (Ethereum, Solana). Zap first, engage second. Round numbers (21, 210, 2100) are culturally meaningful (Bitcoin references).
